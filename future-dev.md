# Future Dev Features: Silent Disco & Live DJ Enhancements

Since DiscoCast (MilkScreen) is going to be bundled with a Silent Disco application, we have an awesome opportunity to make the visualizer an actual performance tool for DJs and a more immersive experience for the dancers. 

Here are some grounded, artistic, and DJ-focused ideas to push the visualizer to the next level:

## 1. 🎧 Multi-Channel Vibe Sync
Silent discos are all about the colored channels (Red, Green, Blue). The visuals should reflect the vibe of the channel that's currently crushing it.
- **Color Overrides:** Instantly tint or wash the entire visualizer in Red, Green, or Blue to match the active channel.
- **Channel Hotkeys:** Press `1` (Red), `2` (Green), or `3` (Blue) to switch to presets that heavily feature those colors, uniting the crowd visually with what they are hearing in their headphones.

## 2. 🎛️ MIDI Controller Integration
Give the visualizer physical tactility so a VJ or DJ can "play" it live.
- **Plug and Play:** Map a standard USB MIDI controller (like an Akai APC or Novation Launchpad) to the app.
- **Live Tweaking:** Use drum pads to trigger specific favorite presets, and use knobs/faders to manually control visual intensity, zoom, or rotation in real-time.

## 3. ⚡ Performance "Hype" Keys
Visuals should hit as hard as the music during peak moments.
- **Strobe & Flash:** Spacebar or MIDI pad shortcuts to flash the screen pure white directly on the kick drum.
- **The Blackout:** Instantly cut everything to black during a long, tense build-up, and release it back into chaotic visuals right on the drop.
- **Color Invert:** A quick toggle to invert all colors for a sudden, jarring visual shift.

## 4. 🎚️ Audio Reactivity Tuning
Different genres drive visuals differently. A heavy bass track needs different visual sensitivity than a vocal house anthem.
- **EQ Isolation:** Sliders to tell the visualizer what to listen to. Crank the bass reactivity for dubstep, or focus on the mid-range so the visuals pulse to the vocals.
- **Intensity Multiplier:** A master "Energy" slider that forces the visualizer's warp and motion speeds to go 2x or 3x faster for high-bpm tracks.

## 5. ⏱️ Live Tempo (BPM) Mapping
- **Beat Sync:** Analyze the audio feed to guess the BPM, and lock the pulsing of the visualizer to that tempo.
- **Groove Control:** A quick toggle to make the visuals react in half-time (great for trap/dubstep) or double-time (great for drum & bass).

## 6. 🗂️ Set-Building (Visual Playlists)
Expand the "Favorites" system into a full set-building tool for DJs.
- **Crates:** Build specific lists of presets for different parts of the night (e.g., "Warmup Room", "Peak Hour Techno", "Sunrise Chillout").
- **Seamless Blending:** Adjust the crossfade time between presets manually so slower sets have long, dreamy 15-second morphs, while hard sets snap instantly.

## 7. 🎨 Custom Silhouettes & Alpha Masks
- **Visual Stencils:** Drop a transparent PNG (like a skull, a peace sign, or a DJ symbol) into the app, and have the MilkDrop visuals wrap around it, interact with its edges, or only show *inside* the shape.
