Overview

What's in the app

Over 1,100 reactive visuals, a full preset studio, a timeline editor for planning shows, and live performance controls — all in one app.

1,144 MilkDrop Presets

The full Butterchurn library plus the community-curated Baron pack — all bundled locally, no network calls needed.

Live Audio Input

Mic, USB DJ controller, external sound card — pick your device from a native dropdown and the visuals follow whatever you're playing.

Auto-Gain & Kick-Lock

AGC keeps the visuals consistently punchy regardless of volume. Kick-Lock isolates the low end so everything syncs to the kick drum.

Projector Ready

Screen Wake Lock, auto-hiding cursor, and Zen Mode (press H) for zero-UI projection. Built for live events and installations.

Live Performance Keys

One-key strobe, blackout, and color inversion. Hold Shift for a 2× intensity boost. Fast enough for live DJ sets.

Preset Browser

Searchable drawer across all 1,144 presets. Heart your favorites, hide the ones you don't want, and cycle through only the good stuff.

Preset Studio

Make your own visuals

The Preset Studio is where you build custom presets from scratch. Pick a color palette, tweak the motion and wave controls, then layer in your own images and make them react to the music.

  • 12 one-click palettes with 3 independent color swatches
  • 5 tabbed sections: Palette, Motion, Wave, Feel, Images
  • 50-deep undo/redo and A/B comparison
  • Saves to localStorage — your presets are always there

Image Layers — the fun part

You can add up to 5 image layers to any custom preset. Upload your own photos, textures, or artwork and the visualizer composites them into the scene in real-time. Each layer has independent controls, so you can build complex layered visuals that react differently to bass, mids, or treble.

Drag-to-reorder layer stack with per-layer solo & mute
Per-layer audio reactivity — choose Bass, Mid, Treble, or Volume
Response curves: Linear, Squared, Cubed, or Gate
UV mirror with Per Tile or Whole Image scope
Auto-resize on upload (1024px standard / 2048px HD toggle)
Aspect-correct tiling — portrait, square, or landscape images tile without distortion
Per-layer rename with static thumbnail preview
Scene-level Canvas Mirror for global symmetry effects
Timeline Editor

Plan a whole show

Arrange presets on a timeline, set durations and blend times, then hit play. The Zone Compositor lets you run multiple presets in different screen regions at the same time.

  • Multi-track strip with drag-to-reorder and snap-to-grid
  • Zone Compositor — quadrant, banner, center, custom rect
  • Per-zone opacity, blend mode, and gap behavior
  • JSON export/import — share shows with other DJs
Controls

Keyboard Controls

The whole UI is designed to stay out of your way — glassmorphic controls auto-hide after 3 seconds, and every action has a keyboard shortcut. No menus to dig through.

  • Live hype keys — strobe, blackout, invert, and Shift for 2× boost
  • Audio tuning — AGC and Kick-Lock toggle with one key
  • Preset nav — arrows, random, favorites, hide unwanted presets
  • Zen Mode — press H to hide all UI for clean projection
  • Quick launch — E for Preset Studio, L for Timeline Editor
